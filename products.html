Vision C
Vision C is a powerful mini-SCADA software that offers a wide range of features. One of its notable features is its support for protocols such as Modbus RTU, AIBUS, and Modbus TCP/IP. With zero development time and cost, Vision C provides a hassle-free experience for users. It also boasts a simple UI with live online trend monitoring.

Additionally, Vision C supports multiple COM ports and Ethernet, allowing for flexible connectivity options. The software ensures secure data recording with encryption and offers the convenience of generating PDF reports. Users can customize their access levels, ensuring a personalized experience. Regular software updates and AI optimizations further enhance the performance of Vision C.

Vision Web
