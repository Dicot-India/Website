Our Vision & Mission

Founded in Ahmedabad, India, Dicot Innovations is a leading Industrial IoT company dedicated to democratizing factory data access. We specialize in SCADA alternatives, data logging software, and remote monitoring solutions, including our flagship products: Vision C, Vision S, and Vision Web. Our mission is to empower manufacturing industries with innovative, cost-effective IoT solutions that enhance digitalization and operational efficiency. Driven by user feedback, we continuously refine our software to set new industry standards, ensuring seamless connectivity and same-day support for our global clients.
